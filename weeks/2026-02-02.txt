Recipes (scaled for 3)

Monday — Crockpot Crack Chicken over Rice
- 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
- ½ oz ranch seasoning (half packet)
- 8 oz cream cheese
- 4 slices bacon, cooked and chopped
- ¾ cup shredded cheddar
- ¼ cup sliced green onions
- 1 cup white rice
- Slow cooker LOW 7 hrs. Shred, stir, top with cheddar, melt 10 min. Serve over rice.

Tuesday — Chicken Burrito Bowls
- 1 lb chicken breast, diced
- 1 Tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp chili powder, ½ tsp garlic powder, salt & pepper
- 1 cup rice
- 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained
- ½ cup corn (frozen or canned)
- ½ cup salsa, ¼ cup sour cream
- Toppings: shredded cheese, avocado, cilantro
- Season and sauté chicken 6–7 min. Serve over rice with beans, corn, and toppings.

Wednesday — GF Fettuccine Alfredo with Broccoli
- 9 oz GF fettuccine
- 2 Tbsp butter, 1 cup heavy cream, 1 clove garlic minced
- ¾ cup grated Parmesan, salt & pepper
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- Melt butter, sauté garlic 30 sec, add cream, simmer 3 min. Stir in Parmesan until smooth. Toss with cooked GF pasta. Steam or roast broccoli on the side.

Thursday — Honey Garlic Salmon with Steamed Green Beans
- 3 salmon fillets (~5 oz each)
- 2 Tbsp honey, 1 Tbsp tamari/GF soy sauce, 1 tsp minced garlic, 1 Tbsp butter
- 12 oz green beans, salt & pepper
- Sear salmon skin-side up 3 min, flip, add honey-garlic-tamari sauce, cook 3 more min basting. Steam green beans on the side.

Friday — Crockpot Chicken Tortilla Soup
- 1 lb chicken breast
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes, 1 can (15 oz) black beans (drained), 1 cup corn
- 1 cup chicken broth, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp chili powder, ½ tsp garlic powder, salt
- Toppings: corn tortilla chips, shredded cheese, sour cream, avocado, cilantro, lime
- Place chicken, tomatoes, beans, corn, broth, and spices in slow cooker. Cook LOW 6–8 hrs. Shred chicken, stir. Serve with toppings.

Saturday — Crockpot Salsa Verde Chicken Tacos
- 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
- 1 cup salsa verde
- 1 tsp cumin, ½ tsp garlic powder, salt
- Corn tortillas (GF)
- Toppings: diced onion, cilantro, lime, shredded cheese, sour cream
- Place chicken in slow cooker, pour salsa verde, season. Cook LOW 6 hrs. Shred and serve in corn tortillas.

Sunday — Teriyaki Chicken Rice Bowls
- 1 lb chicken thighs, sliced
- Sauce: 3 Tbsp GF soy sauce/tamari, 1 Tbsp honey, 1 tsp sesame oil, 1 tsp rice vinegar, 1 tsp cornstarch + 1 Tbsp water
- 1 cup rice
- 1 cup steamed broccoli or snap peas
- Sauté chicken 6 min, add sauce, cook until thickened. Serve over rice with veggies. Top with sesame seeds and green onion.
