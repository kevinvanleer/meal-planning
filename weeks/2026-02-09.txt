Recipes (scaled for 3)

Monday — Steak Fajita Rice Bowls
- ¾ lb flank or skirt steak, sliced thin
- 1 bell pepper, sliced; ½ onion, sliced
- 1 Tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp chili powder, ½ tsp garlic powder, salt & pepper
- 1 cup rice
- Toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, avocado, lime
- Sear steak strips 2–3 min over high heat, remove. Sauté peppers and onions 5 min. Serve over rice with toppings.

Tuesday — Crockpot Beef Stew (GF)
- 1 lb beef stew meat, cubed
- 3 medium potatoes, cubed; 2 carrots, sliced; 1 stalk celery, sliced; ½ onion, diced
- 2 cups beef broth, 2 Tbsp tomato paste, 1 tsp Worcestershire (check GF), 1 tsp garlic powder, salt & pepper
- 1 Tbsp cornstarch + 1 Tbsp water (to thicken at end)
- Brown beef in skillet 3 min. Add everything to slow cooker. Cook LOW 8 hrs. Stir in cornstarch slurry, cook 10 min more.

Wednesday — Marinated Pork Tenderloin with Roasted Sweet Potato
- 1 lb pork tenderloin
- Marinade: 3 Tbsp olive oil, 1 Tbsp brown sugar, 1 tsp Dijon, 1 Tbsp tamari, ½ tsp lemon zest, 1 tsp each parsley & thyme, ½ tsp garlic, salt & pepper
- 2 medium sweet potatoes, cubed, tossed with olive oil, salt, pepper
- Marinate 1–8 hrs. Grill or sauté to 145°F (~15 min). Rest 5 min. Roast sweet potatoes at 400°F for 25 min.

Thursday — Lemon Herb Chicken with Roasted Asparagus
- 3 boneless skinless chicken thighs (~1 lb)
- 2 Tbsp olive oil, juice of 1 lemon, 1 tsp dried oregano, 1 tsp garlic powder, salt & pepper
- 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed, tossed with olive oil, salt, pepper
- Marinate chicken 30 min. Sear in skillet 5–6 min per side. Roast asparagus at 400°F for 12 min.

Friday — Shrimp Tacos (corn tortillas)
- ¾ lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 Tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp chili powder, ½ tsp cumin, ½ tsp garlic powder, salt
- Corn tortillas (GF)
- Slaw: 2 cups shredded cabbage, 2 Tbsp mayo, 1 Tbsp lime juice, pinch of salt
- Toppings: avocado, cilantro, lime wedges, hot sauce
- Sauté shrimp 2–3 min per side. Mix slaw. Serve in warmed corn tortillas with slaw and toppings.

Saturday — Crockpot Italian Sausage & Peppers over Rice
- 1 lb Italian sausage links (GF — check label)
- 2 bell peppers, sliced; ½ onion, sliced
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, ½ tsp garlic powder, salt & pepper
- 1 cup rice
- Place sausage, peppers, onions, tomatoes, and seasoning in slow cooker. Cook LOW 6 hrs. Slice sausage. Serve over rice.

Sunday — GF Fettuccine Alfredo with Garlic Butter Broccoli
- 9 oz GF fettuccine
- 2 Tbsp butter, 1 cup heavy cream, 1 clove garlic minced
- ¾ cup grated Parmesan, salt & pepper
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- Melt butter, sauté garlic 30 sec, add cream, simmer 3 min. Stir in Parmesan until smooth. Toss with cooked GF pasta. Steam or roast broccoli on the side.
